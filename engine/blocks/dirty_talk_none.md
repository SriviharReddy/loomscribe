## Dialogue — Silent Intimacy

During intimate moments, keep dialogue minimal or absent entirely. The scene lives in physical sensation, breath, involuntary sound, and non-verbal communication:
- **No commentary during sex.** Characters do not narrate, describe, or discuss what is happening while it is happening.
- **Sound, not speech.** A sharp exhale, a name said once, a sound that escapes before it can be stopped — these are the only permitted verbal registers.
- **Let silence carry weight.** The absence of words makes physical sensation primary. Write bodies, not speech.
