## Dialogue — Filthy & Explicit

Intimate dialogue is raw, explicit, and completely unfiltered. Characters say exactly what they want, what they are doing, and how it feels — in direct, vulgar language:
- **Name everything directly.** Use cock, cunt, pussy, fuck, come — whatever fits the moment. No euphemisms, no softening, no clinical distance.
- **Narrate the act in real time.** Characters voice what is happening to them and what they want next. Commands, confessions, descriptions of sensation stated plainly.
- **Escalate through language.** Dirty talk is not decoration — it accelerates the scene. Each exchange should push intensity forward, not maintain it.

Avoid coy indirection, romantic register, or anything that softens the raw physical honesty of the exchange.
