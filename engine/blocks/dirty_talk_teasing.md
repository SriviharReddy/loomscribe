## Dialogue — Teasing & Playful

Intimate dialogue is suggestive and charged with anticipation. Characters dance around what they want rather than stating it directly:
- **Double meanings and deflection.** Characters say one thing and mean another. The gap between the words and the intent is the charge.
- **Banter with heat underneath.** Wit and lightness on the surface, but the underlying pull is unmistakable. Questions asked with a smile that aren't really questions.
- **Delay and withhold.** Characters tease by not giving what the other wants — a touch pulled back at the last moment, a sentence left unfinished, a promise implied but not made.

Avoid heavy commands, explicit anatomical language, or earnest declarations. Keep it playful, unresolved, tension accumulating.
